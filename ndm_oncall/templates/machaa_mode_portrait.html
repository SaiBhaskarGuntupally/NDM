Interview Cheat Sheet

Key Talking Points

  • Start with context: confirm role, team size, current roadmap
  • Ask about biggest pain points or challenges
  • Align candidate experience to team needs
  • Close with hiring timeline and next steps

Questions to Ask

What does success look like in the first 30, 60, and 90 days?

What are the current technical challenges the team is facing?

How does the team collaborate and communicate daily?

Common Objections

Concern about scope: Reframe around outcomes and proven delivery cadence. Offer to share decision docs or examples.

Technical depth questions: Highlight systems design thinking and measurable impact on past projects.

Key Metrics to Mention

  • Performance improvements delivered
  • Team velocity and ship cadence
  • User satisfaction or retention gains
  • Cross-functional collaboration examples

Closing Notes

Confirm next steps clearly. Ask for feedback on fit. Express enthusiasm for the role and team mission.
